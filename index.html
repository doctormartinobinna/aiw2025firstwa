About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research – Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q2

This hands‑on 8‑week program is designed to equip professionals and researchers with practical skills to integrate AI into daily workflows without any coding required. By the end of the bootcamp, you’ll be able to:

  • Create polished websites, documents, and presentations with AI assistance
  • Perform literature reviews, analyze data, and build intuitive dashboards
  • Design custom chatbots capable of answering domain‑specific questions
  • Set up automated workflows that run 24/7
  • Produce and edit images, videos, and design materials with AI tools
  • Develop practical AI solutions that accelerate your day‑to‑day projects
